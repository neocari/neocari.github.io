Parts of a Guitar

There are many parts of a guitar, and understanding them can help you in maintaining and taking care of your guitar properly.

  • Headstock
  • Tuning Pegs
  • Nut (white part separating headstock and neck)
  • Fretboard
  • Frets
  • Neck
  • Body
  • Bridge
  • Pickups (only for electric guitars)
  • Strings

Guitar Chords

The essential chords for beginners, ordered from easiest to hardest include:

  1. A major
  2. D major
  3. C major
  4. E major
  5. G major
  6. B major
  7. F major

It is very important to learn every chord shape as you go along your guitar journey, not just the beginner ones.


Reputable Guitar Brands

When buying a guitar, it's important that you buy from a reputable brands. These brands are known for their high-quality guitars:

  • Fender
  • Gibson
  • Ibanez
  • PRS (Paul Reed Smith)
  • Yamaha
  • Martin
  • Gretsch
  • Jackson
