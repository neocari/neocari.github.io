Parts of a Guitar

There are many parts of a guitar, and understanding them can help you in maintaining and taking care of your guitar properly.

  • Headstock
  • Tuning Pegs
  • Nut (white part separating headstock and neck)
  • Fretboard
  • Frets
  • Neck
  • Body
  • Bridge
  • Pickups (only for electric guitars)
  • Strings
